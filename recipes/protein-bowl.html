Protein Bowl

Description

Perfect recipe for when you need a quick and protein-packed breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 70 g turkey breast slices
  • 100 g low fat cottage cheese
  • 1 whole apple

Steps

  1. Prepare the egg (boil, fry or poach it, whichever is your favorite).
  2. Slice the apple.
  3. Arrange everything in a bowl.
  4. Eat!
