Whey

Description

Perfect to add extra protein in smoothies!

Ingredients

  • 2 liter whole milk
  • 1 ml rennet
  • digital thermometer
  • strain and a clean cloth

Steps

  1. Heat milk up to 36 or 37 degrees celsius.
  2. Add the rennet, let rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Cut the milk with a knife, let rest again for another 10 minutes.
  4. Pour over the cloth (which should be placed in a strain). Make sure you have a container under that will collect the liquid (whey).
  5. Bottle the whey and store in fridge.
  6. What's left in the cloth can be used to make mozzarella!
